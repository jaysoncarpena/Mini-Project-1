1 on 1 Mentorship


“One of the greatest values of mentors is the ability to see ahead what others cannot see and to help them navigate a course to their destination.”

— John C. Maxwell

Why? We all need people in our lives to intentionally help us navigate life. Our mentors have compiled knowledge and wisdom through their diverse athletic, educational and life experiences and have been where you want to go. It is our belief that having caring role models in our corner can make a substantial positive impact on our life journey. We are here to inspire, encourage and empower you in your unique life stage.

Who? Middle school, high school and university aged athletes who want guidance in their education, basketball or overall life journey.

What?

Program Pillars

1.Passion- Identify major areas of interest to customize activities, content and conversations that suit your needs.

2.Purpose- Get to the root of your main motivators so you can tap into those to further your growth.

3.Practice- Establish daily habits that will help provide opportunities that align with your passion purpose. Program includes 4 weeks of mentorship, support and accountability. Mentees will benefit from weekly online meetings (or in person at your request) and text check-ins.

How?

- Choose your start date! Registration is ongoing.

- Participation in the program can be renewed each month to continue an extended mentorship.
